Title Placement

For each option in Section 1 (Column Layout) an additional option is available in which the entire text is positionend to the right of an image. The image height can be chosen freely, but some restrictions apply to the image widths for the respective column layouts.

Predefined configurations:

  • 1-column layout: 200px, 160px, 120px, 100px, 80px, 60px, 40px
  • 2-column layout: 100px, 80px, 60px, 40px, 20px
  • 3-column layout: 60px, 40px, 20px, 10px
