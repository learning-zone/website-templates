Welcome to Leaves.

Welcome to Leaves, a static, 3 column layout made with your usual CSS and XHTML. It is able to correctly accommodate any font size increases or shrinkages (Is that a word?). It seems to work fine in Firefox, Opera, Internet Explorer and Safari. It's more minimal than other designs, because I think images (drop shadows, giant header images) are being obsessively over used these days. I think it detracts from the content and shoves way too much information to a viewer all at the same time, so here you go: Autumn, a minimalist design. Feel free to massacre or change the design to your liking. Well, I guess it's time for some more Latin. Feel free to drop me a line anytime.
